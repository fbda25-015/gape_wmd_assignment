Our Loan Facilities

Tailored financial solutions to empower every sector of the Botswana economy.

Agribusiness Loans

Funding for crop production, livestock, and agro-processing. Special focus on food security and commercial farming.

  • Rate: 2.75% - 5.75%
  • Max: P 50 Million

Manufacturing Loans

Supporting value-addition industries including textiles, food processing, and chemical production.

  • Rate: 2.75% - 5.75%
  • Max: P 50 Million

Real Estate & Construction

Funding for commercial property development, retail spaces, and construction projects across Botswana.

  • Rate: 4.25% - 5.75%
  • Repayment: Up to 20 years

Trade Finance Facilities

Short-term working capital solutions including order financing, invoice discounting, and performance bonds.

  • Fast-track processing
  • Project-based funding

Services & Tourism

Funding for lodges, travel agencies, transport services, and professional consulting firms.

  • Rate: 2.75% (Tourism focus)
  • Max: P 50 Million

Creative & Digital

Tailored for the arts, media production, and technology start-ups to drive innovation.

  • Subsidized rates
  • Asset & Working capital
