How to Apply

A straightforward, step-by-step guide to securing CEDA funding—from eligibility to disbursement.

Are You Eligible?

Botswana Citizen

Aged 18 years or older with a valid Omang.

Citizen Ownership

100% or majority citizen-owned businesses.

Business Registration

Legally registered business (CIPA) in a supported sector.

Good Credit Standing

A clean financial history is required for assessment.

Application Timelines

Micro/Small 4–8 weeks
Medium 8–16 weeks
Large-Scale 3–6 months

*Timelines depend on document completeness and project complexity.

Your Path to Funding

01

Business Plan

Develop a comprehensive plan including market analysis, 3-year financial projections, and operational strategy.

02

Gather Documents

  • Certified Omang
  • CIPA Certificate
  • Tax Clearance (BURS)
  • Bank Statements (6mo)
03

Submit & Track

Submit via www.ceda.co.bw. Use the online portal to track status and communicate with advisors anytime.

Pro Tips for Success

Be Realistic

Base projections on solid research. Over-optimism is a leading cause of rejection.

Show Commitment

Demonstrate "skin in the game" through personal contributions or in-kind assets.

Job Creation

Highlight how your business will contribute to local employment and the economy.

Ready to Take the First Step?

Apply online through our CEDA portal or visit your nearest office — Building Botswana for Batswana
